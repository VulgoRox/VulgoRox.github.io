Personal Data We Process

Depending on how you use Estilo BR, we may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information, such as identifiers, display name, public profile details, email address, profile photo, and authentication tokens received from supported login providers.
  • Gameplay and backend data, such as player ID, inventory, progress, purchases, bans, multiplayer/session information, crash logs, support records, device data, IP address, and fraud-prevention signals.
  • Analytics data, such as device identifiers, usage events, session activity, approximate location derived from IP address, browser/app information, and cookie or tracking technology data where applicable.
  • Payment-related data, such as transaction status, order identifiers, purchased items, receipt data, and limited payment confirmation information. Estilo BR does not receive full card numbers from Google Pay.
  • Social feature data, such as public profile information and content or interactions that you choose to make visible to other users.
